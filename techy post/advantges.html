Advantges of Quantum Computing

AI and Machine Learning

Machine learning is about computers learning from sets of data and then being able to carry out tasks on their own by analyzing repeating patterns in the data, in a fashion which is much similar to the human brain. Quantum computing can significantly enhance the creation and analyzation of these patterns of data and the inputted data.

Cryptography and security

Cryptography and security are about securing data from unauthorized access. Quantum computing can potentially take cryptography and security to another level by making data much harder to access from a hacker's point of view. As quantum computing uses qubits it can calculate all the possible ways of data breach attempts and provide appropriate data to fortify the information.

Route and traffic optimization

Optimal route planning is key to smooth supply chain logistics and transportation. The biggest challenge that we confrot with classical computers is that they are unable to procces the large amounts of data consisting of weather patterns, traffic congestion, etc. Problems which can be easily resolved due with the help of quantum computing.

Drug and Chemical research

Drug discovery is the process of developing a drug from an initial hypothesis to a fully commercialized product. It is an another area in which their is great promise for the utilisation of quantum computing. As a prominent example, quantum simulation will enable faster and more accurate characterizations of molecular systems than existing chemistry methods.

Manufacturing

Another potential application of quantum computing lies in the field of manufacturing. Where we can use quantum computers to accurately process large amounts of data to help in logistics and supply. We can also use quantum computing to design and implement new concepts and design, thanks to quantum computers ability to enhance our machine learning abilities.
